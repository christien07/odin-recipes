Lasagna

Description

An excellent choice for dinner, this lasagna will be prepared with ground beef, Italian seasonings, and fresh basil leaves.

Set aside a couple hours to complete this meal for 10!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 16oz lasagna noodles
  • One onion, chopped
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1Tbsp Italian seasoning
  • 4 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 cup Romano cheese
  • 2.5 cups of spaghetti sauce
  • 1.5 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 medium zucchini, sliced
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves

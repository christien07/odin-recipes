Lemonade Recipe

Back to Home Page

Description

If you ever needed a cool glass of refreshing lemonade for a working day, here is the recipe for you.

Only four ingredients for perfection, served with brown sugar. Only one step to make it easy.

Ingredients

  • 6 lemons, juiced
  • 1.5 cups water
  • 0.5 cups brown sugar
  • 0.5 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Step 1: In a large pitcher, combine fresh lemon juice and water. Stir in brown sugar until it dissolves. Stir in ice cubes.
